Joy

We believe everyone deserves to have plants in their home and to share a space with organic entities - ones that promote tranquilty and improve overall wellbeing. But it's not always easy to remember when you last watered something, or to know when it's time to fertilize. Botanist can handle everything from watering and fertilizing schedules, soil assessment, adaptive supplementary lighting, pruning, and even offer encouraging music in it's free time.

Connection

We know the frustration of bringing home a plant and watching it slowly wilt and die but not understanding what you did wrong. We also don't want to take away all the responsibility, because we know how important it is to care for something and celebrate it's growth. That's why Botanist is a helper if that's all you need, or your green-thumbed roommate that cares for the plants while you sit back and enjoy their beauty! Our easy to use app is all you need to help your bot understand how it can help.
